How to play flappy bird:

  1. Press the green flag
  2. Use space key to bounce up, the longer you wait until the next time you press the key, the further down the bird falls
  3. Be sure to not touch pipes as you glide through them
  4. How far can you make it?

Here is how to play Falling object game, win at 30

  1. Press the green flag
  2. Use arrow keys to move left and right
  3. Race to catch the falling oranges
  4. Can you make it to 30?
